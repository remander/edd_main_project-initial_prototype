Track your posture easier than ever before

Many individuals today suffer from chronic back pain and poor posture. The multifidus muscle, a key stabilizer of the spine, plays a critical role in maintaining posture and core stability. This project aims to develop a wearable sensor that continuously monitors the activity of the multifidus muscle, providing real-time feedback to users about their posture and muscle engagement.
