Step 1: Sensor Setup Guide

Important steps:

  1. Ensure surface electrodes are properly attached in reference to the multifidus muscle.
  2. Put on back brace comfortably
  3. Place a circuit box on both sides of the back brace using the built-in clip system
  4. Make sure the each sensor complex is plugged in and connected to the surface elctrodes

Step 2: Calibration Process

Calibration steps:

  1. Connect ESP-32 and turn on both MyoWare EMG Sensors
  2. Ensure the ESP-32 is uploading code
  3. Stand in a neutral position for around 30 seconds while ESP connects to MyoWare sensors and runs setup code
  4. Wait for the blue light to turn on, indicating a successful connection
  5. Ensure that the ESP-32 is sending data

Step 3: Monitoring

Monitoring:

  1. Ensure that you have an account and you are logged in
  2. Use the sensor during everyday activities
  3. Monitor your posture and muscle activity through the web portal

Frequently Asked Questions

The goal of PosturePro is to monitor the health and activity of the multifidus muscle. We identified this muscle as a key stabilizer in the spine, and targeting the area will help protect and provide information about general posture health. The project itself provides users with real-time data to raise awareness of their posture.

As a key muscle for supporting the spinal column, the health of multifidus is closely related to the surrounding muscles. Therefore, dysfunction or atrophy of this muscle is strongly linked to lower back pain and poor posture. By targeting this muscle, PosturePro addresses the root cause of many posture-related problems.

PosturePro is designed for anyone experiencing back pain or poor posture. This includes the elderly or inactive individuals—but can also be beneficial for athletes, office workers, or anyone interested in improving their posture.

The sensor system is designed to be worn comfortably in a back brace that holds the electrodes in place. Adhesive skin electrodes are placed on the skin over key points of the multifidus muscle. The over design is intended to stay secure while performing small exercises.

The sensor connects wirelessly to a website portal, where users can view live data, see their past trends, and compare with example data. The portal is designed to be intuitive and easy to use, even for non-technical users.
